## How to sign

The Project uses an automated CLA assistant. When you open your first pull request, a bot will
comment with a link. Click it, review this agreement, and confirm — your GitHub identity is
recorded as your signature. The bot re-checks every subsequent PR automatically, so you only
sign once.

If the automated flow is unavailable, you may sign by replying to your pull request with the
exact sentence:

> I have read the CLA Document and I hereby sign the CLA.

Your GitHub username, the PR, and the timestamp constitute your signature record.
